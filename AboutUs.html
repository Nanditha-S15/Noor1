Our Inspiration

The idea for Noor came from our own struggles with finding clothes that truly fit and flatter...

We also noticed another challenge: colors and styles that look stunning on a mannequin often don’t suit everyone’s skin tone, body shape, or personality...

That’s when we thought of Noor — a platform that connects people to experienced fashion designers and stylists...

At Noor, our inspiration is simple: to make fashion personal, effortless, and perfectly you.

"Noor was born out of my own frustration with finding clothes that truly fit and felt right. Shopping often turned into a struggle...I still remember buying a so-called “ready-to-wear” kurti that ended up needing to be completely restitched before I could wear it."

—Avishi, Co-Founder & CEO, Noor
